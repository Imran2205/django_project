How to Buy Bitcoin with Credit Card or Debit Card

Getting Started

Welcome to Paybis, the one-stop-shop for all your cryptocurrency (and digital currency) needs. 
Here is a short guide on how to buy Bitcoin with credit card or debit card: 

  • 1st Step: Create a Bitcoin wallet—we suggest using Electrum.
  • 2nd Step: Start a new transaction on Paybis.
  • 3rd Step: Enter your BTC address.
  • 4rth Step: Pay with your card.

The whole process takes 15 minutes (or less) to complete. To learn more about buying Bitcoin, swipe to the next step.

What is Bitcoin and How Does it Work?

Since its creation in 2009, Bitcoin has become the most prominent method to store and exchange value across the web:

  • You can buy and send Bitcoin all over the world at all times.
  • Transfers are cheaper and much faster than bank transfers.
  • Bitcoin is fairly private, and it is not controlled by 3rd parties.
  • Furthermore, Bitcoin has a limited supply of 21 million coins.

Thanks to all of this, Bitcoin’s value tends to appreciate over time.

Why use a Bank Card to Buy Bitcoin?

Credit and debit cards are the most popular payment method when buying goods and services worldwide. That’s also the reason why people favor bank cards over other payment methods. 
Therefore, when looking for the best option to buy Bitcoin instantly, look no further—choose debit cards or credit cards. They are the fastest and most secure way to buy BTC instantaneously in your local currency.

Choosing a Bitcoin Wallet

To buy Bitcoin with debit card or credit card, you will need to create a Bitcoin wallet, and here are your options:

  • Desktop wallet - Easy to use and very secure. The most popular option is Electrum.
  • Hardware wallet - Outstanding security. The most popular option is Ledger.
  • Online wallet - Very convenient.
  • Paper wallet - Budget option that’s also secure.

In short, the best wallet to buy Bitcoin instantly is Electrum because it’s safe and takes just a minute to create.

How to Create a Wallet

Next, you need to create a Bitcoin wallet, and here are the steps to follow:

  • 1st Step: Download Electrum wallet
  • 2nd Step: Install the software and create a new wallet.
  • 3rd Step: Go to the “Receive” section and copy your Bitcoin wallet address.
Now, you need to place your order on Paybis. So, to understand the process, read the "step-by-step guide" shown below.
After you purchase Bitcoin, there are several things you can do with your coins.

What Can You Do With Your Bitcoin

Once you buy BTC with credit card or debit card and become a BTC owner, here are your options:

  • Hold onto your Bitcoin (remember, to keep BTC safe, don’t share the private key with anyone).
  • Buy goods from businesses that accept Bitcoin payments.
  • Exchange your BTC with other cryptocurrencies.
  • Transfer Bitcoin to friends, family, or charities.

These are the Bitcoin basics! You can now proceed with the purchase.

A step-by-step guide to buying Cryptocurrency on Paybis

  1. When you choose to buy Bitcoin with credit card or debit card, you will need to do the following:
    • Enter the amount you wish to exchange into Bitcoin value. 
    • After that’s done, click the “Start Transaction” button to buy BTC with debit card or credit card.
  2. If you are buying BTC with card for the first time, you will need to verify your identity: 
    • Enter your first and last name, as well as your date of birth, then click on “Continue”.
    • At this point, please enter your address and click on “Continue” once again.
    • Finally, upload a digital copy of your ID, click on “Submit” and wait for a few minutes while our team reviews your submitted document.
  3. Less than 5 minutes later, your verification will either be approved or rejected:
    • In case of rejection, our system will indicate which mistakes need to be fixed. You will also receive an email as an additional reminder.
    • If your account is successfully verified, you will need to enter the address of your Bitcoin wallet. This will let us know where you wish your funds to be sent.

  4. Once you enter your BTC wallet address, click on “Continue”. You will be redirected to our payment processor’s secure checkout page.

    • Follow the instructions and provide the necessary information.
    • Make a payment to complete your purchase.

  5. Once your card has been validated and processed, your order is complete! Verification and processing usually take an additional 5-10 minutes:

    • Please note that your payment might require a manual review. As such, monitor your email inbox for an additional verification email.
    • If the delivery of your BTC takes longer than expected, please contact our support team. We stand ready 24/7 to help you out with any issues that may arise.
    And that’s it. These are the exact steps you need to take to buy Bitcoin instantly with credit card or debit card.

People often struggle to understand that there is a limited supply of Bitcoins and that it will only be possible to mine a total of 21 million Bitcoins.
With traditional money, it is possible to print more when it’s necessary. That’s the main reason why it loses its value over time.
Bitcoin, in this way, is different. Its supply is fixed, which, in turn, increases its value over time.
Here are a few more facts about Bitcoin:

  • How many Bitcoins have been mined? - Today, there are more than 18 million Bitcoins, and each day, about 1,800 more are mined.
  • How is Bitcoin created? - Bitcoin is created by computers that solve complex mathematical equations, adding more and more bitcoins to the blockchain.
  • How much Bitcoin does Satoshi have? - Researchers have estimated that Satoshi mined up to 700,000 Bitcoins back in 2009.
